How it works

From install to the first blink in under five minutes.

Create your account

Sign up with your email address or your Google account — on Android or right in the browser.

Connect your light

Log in to Philips Hue once and choose the lamp you want to share.

Add your friends

Send an invite link. When your friend accepts, you appear on each other's home screen.

Blink away

Tap a friend's tile and their lamp blinks in your color. That's it — that's the app.
